    How does the ELEVATE posture corrector work?

    The Elevate Posture Corrector gently aligns your shoulders and spine to improve your posture. Its adjustable straps ensure a comfortable fit, while consistent use trains your muscles to maintain better posture naturally. By providing support, it helps alleviate back pain and discomfort caused by poor posture. Designed for daily wear, it can be used under or over clothing for convenience.

    Is the ELEVATE posture corrector visible under clothes?

    The Elevate Posture Corrector is designed with discretion in mind, making it virtually invisible under clothing. Its sleek, lightweight material and adjustable straps ensure a snug fit without bulk, allowing you to wear it comfortably throughout the day. Whether worn under a shirt or blouse, it remains discreet, so you can maintain good posture without compromising your style.

    When should I wear the ELEVATE posture corrector?

    For optimal results, wear the Elevate Posture Corrector during daily activities such as working at your desk, driving, or while relaxing at home. It’s most effective when used for 1-2 hours a day initially, gradually increasing as your posture improves. Consistent wear helps reinforce good posture habits and alleviate discomfort throughout your daily routine.

    How long does the shipping take?

    Shipping typically takes 1-2 weeks to ensure your posture corrector arrives promptly and in perfect condition.
